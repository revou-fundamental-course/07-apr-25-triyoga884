Profile

Rhodes Island is a large landship consisting of six decks capable of holding a large amount of crew within her hull, allowing to mobilize the personnel anywhere, at any time. In simple terms, it works similar to an aircraft carrier, while serving as a living space for Operators. Because Rhodes Island needs to constantly obtain enough sufficient data to systematically study Oripathy and its effects, the landship also needs to move from area to area. This, however, comes with a cost; Whenever the Rhodes Island moves, Operators generally use ear muffs to relieve the constant, high-intensity noise, in addition to adapt to any region she might encounter â€” be it blizzards in the north or thunderstorms in the south.

Rhodes Island herself is already a mechanical wonder, even compared to high-speed warships. Her size and weight are comparable to a large flagship, only surpassed by Blacksteel's Fort Barron. She has a capacity limit for 2,000 onboard Operators and personnel, and available beds for more than 1,000 patients. The living quarters and crew cabins are available in single, double, and quadruple options, while the exact allocation will be provided for newly-onboard Operators by the HR Department once they receive the approval, and they will be guided by the Logistics staff. In case an Operator doesn't like or isn't used to the bumpy nature of the landship, they can also submit an application to be relocated to any of R.I.'s fixed branch offices. In addition to helipads and farms, she also has a training ground which includes at least one shooting range for the Rhodes Island paramilitary force, and several incineration (or more likely, cremation) chambers to safely dispose of terminally ill Infected R.I. personnel.
